Big Triangle, 2023

When I was in middle school, I doodled a half colored-in triangle on my wrist. Since then, I have made it a bit of a personal logo (see the icon for this website). I drew it on the back of all of my inline skates, and wanted to display it in a bigger way. This wall piece is me trying to do that. I started with a cardboard silhouette and gradually built the actual piece on top of it. It took me about a year to complete, and I ended up finishing it while recovering from a concussion.
